Knowledge Level Courses

Novice

In this phase, learners have a limited but essential vocabulary, often focusing on everyday words and expressions. They can understand and use basic greetings, numbers, and simple conversational phrases.

Intermediate

For this level, learners can write basic sentences and short paragraphs, conveying simple ideas and information. They have a basic awareness of the cultural context associated with the language they are learning.

Advanced

Individuals at this level possess a deep understanding of the cultural nuances associated with the language. They can easily switch between languages and adapt to different linguistic and cultural contexts
